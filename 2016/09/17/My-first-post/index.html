Long time ago, I intented to create my own static blog on github. I tried jekyll and octopress at beginning of this year. Hoever it just not the feel of blog. It kind of short simplicity. I am not against to use ruby. As a Front end developer now, I used ruby quite alot for sass and compass. I also used leanote and wiznote for personal blog building. Still I feels there is something not correct.

Unti I find vimWiki, I realized theen laguage for bloging is not convient for me. As a programmer, I prefer to note down my thoughts as fast as possible. Markdown with TOC seems is perfect for me. Then I recorded jekyll also support markdown. After the second time of investigation in jekyll, I found out jekyll is not convinient for markdown indexing and generating TOC. I know They can be archieve by installing generators. Their hexo falls into my eyes. It’s combination of all functions I am looking. And it’s build on Nodejs, this is a plus for me.
